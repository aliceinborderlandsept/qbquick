Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common QuickBooks questions

How do I backup my QuickBooks company file?

To backup your QuickBooks company file, go to File > Back Up Company > Create Local Backup. You can save it to your computer, an external drive, or cloud storage. We recommend creating regular backups to prevent data loss.

Why is my QuickBooks running slow?

QuickBooks performance can be affected by several factors including large company file size, insufficient system resources, network issues, or data corruption. We can help diagnose the specific cause and optimize your QuickBooks performance.

How do I resolve common QuickBooks error codes?

Common QuickBooks errors like 6000, 6150, or H202 have specific troubleshooting steps. Our technicians are trained to resolve these errors quickly. Contact us with your specific error code for immediate assistance.

Can you help with QuickBooks payroll setup?

Yes, we specialize in QuickBooks payroll setup and troubleshooting. We can help you configure payroll items, tax tables, direct deposit, and ensure compliance with payroll regulations.

What's the difference between QuickBooks Online and Desktop?

QuickBooks Desktop is installed on your computer and offers more advanced features for specific industries. QuickBooks Online is cloud-based, accessible from anywhere, and offers automatic updates. We can help you choose the right version for your business needs.
